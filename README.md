
# Customer Pulse

## Early Behavior Shift & Retention Intelligence Platform

_(Inspired by large FMCG ecosystems like Nestlé)_

##  The Story

Imagine you are a Senior Data Scientist working at a global FMCG company like **Nestlé**.

Every week, leadership reviews dashboards:

- Revenue trends look stable.
- Distribution is expanding.
- Promotions are performing decently.

Yet suddenly — quarterly numbers dip.

No major supply issue.  
No major competitor launch.  
No macro shock.

So what happened?

When you dig deeper, you notice something subtle:

- Purchase frequency started declining **8 weeks ago**.
- Basket sizes quietly reduced.
- Promo usage increased.
- Engagement on the app declined.
- Support tickets slightly increased.

But no system flagged it.
Customers didn’t churn overnight.
They drifted away silently.

##  The Core Business Problem

Large FMCG companies like Nestlé operate across:

- Thousands of SKUs
- Multiple retail channels
- E-commerce
- Distributor networks
- Loyalty programs
- Marketing campaigns
- Support operations

Customer behavior is influenced by:

- Price changes
- Promotions
- Seasonal trends
- Inflation
- Weather
- Campaign exposure
- Service quality

Yet most retention systems are:

- Reactive
- Focused only on churn labels
- Blind to micro behavioral shifts
- Unable to explain root causes
- Not connected to marketing actions

By the time churn probability is high,  
the damage is already done.

## The Opportunity

What if we could detect behavior shift **before churn probability spikes**?

What if we could:

- Detect subtle drops in purchase frequency?
- Identify rising price sensitivity?
- Notice category preference drift?
- Observe declining engagement?
- Connect support issues to retention risk?
- Understand whether the issue is local (store-level) or macro (inflation/weather)?

And most importantly —

What if we could act before revenue declines?

# Project Objective

To design and build a **synthetic, yet realistic FMCG ecosystem simulator** and an **Early Behavior Shift Intelligence Platform** that:

1. Simulates 10,000 customers over 3 years in a Nestlé-like environment
2. Models:
    - Seasonality
    - Promotions
    - Price elasticity
    - Channel mix
    - Support tickets
    - Marketing touches
    - Weather & inflation
3. Introduces realistic pre-churn behavioral drift
4. Detects early micro-behavior changes
5. Scores risk dynamically
6. Diagnoses root cause
7. Suggests intervention strategy

#  The Key Innovation

Traditional churn models answer:

> “Will this customer churn?”

Customer Pulse answers:

> “Is this customer drifting — and why?”

It detects:

- 30–70% drop in order rate over 6 weeks
- Basket contraction patterns
- Rising discount dependency
- Engagement collapse
- Support dissatisfaction clusters

Before inactivity threshold is reached.

#  What This Enables (In a Nestlé-like Setting)

The system allows:

- Identifying at-risk high CLV customers
- Segment-specific intervention strategies
- Campaign personalization
- Promo optimization
- Early inventory planning adjustments
- Store-level anomaly detection
- Risk communication to leadership

Instead of:

> “Revenue dropped last month.”

You can say:

> “We observed a 12% early behavioral drift in the South region, primarily among promo-driven customers in Dairy. Likely influenced by reduced campaign intensity and rising CPI.”

That is strategic intelligence.

#  Why Synthetic?

Public datasets do not:

- Model gradual churn drift
- Connect marketing + support + transactions
- Simulate macro factors
- Provide controlled experimentation environment

This simulator:

- Enables testing retention strategies safely
- Allows experimentation with elasticity
- Provides explainable drift
- Creates reproducible research conditions
